Privacy Policy

Your privacy and the privacy of your students are important to us. This policy explains what data SchoolTrack collects, how it's stored, and how we protect it.

🔒 Your school data is stored locally in your browser — not on our servers. We never see your students' information.

1. Data We Collect

SchoolTrack stores all data locally in your browser's local storage. This includes:

  • Administrator account information (name, school name, email, password)
  • Student records (names, dates of birth, contact details, enrollment status)
  • Academic records (grades, attendance, Hifz progress)
  • Messages and announcements

This data is stored only on your device. SchoolTrack does not transmit this data to any external servers or third parties.

2. How Data Is Stored

All data is stored using your browser's localStorage API. This means:

  • Data persists until you clear your browser data or use the "Reset All Data" feature.
  • Data is not synced across devices or browsers.
  • Different browsers on the same device store separate data.

We strongly recommend using the Export Data feature regularly to maintain offline backups of your school data.

3. Student Data and Privacy Laws

As a school administrator, you are responsible for ensuring that your use of SchoolTrack complies with applicable student privacy laws in your jurisdiction (such as FERPA, COPPA, or GDPR). Because data is stored locally on your device, you maintain direct control over all student information.

4. Cookies and Tracking

SchoolTrack does not use cookies or any tracking technologies. We do not use analytics services, advertising networks, or any third-party scripts that could track your users.

5. Data Security

Your data security depends on the security of your device and browser. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system and browser up to date.
  • Using a strong password for your device and browser.
  • Logging out of SchoolTrack when using shared devices.
  • Exporting and securely storing regular data backups.

6. Data Deletion

You can permanently delete all your data at any time using the Settings → Reset All Data option within the app. Clearing your browser's localStorage will also remove all SchoolTrack data from your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last revised. Continued use of SchoolTrack after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about your privacy, please contact us.
