Cookie Policy

SchoolTrack does not use cookies.

SchoolTrack stores your school data using your browser's localStorage API, which is different from cookies. localStorage data is never sent to servers and is not accessible to third parties.

We do not use any tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies. There are no third-party scripts running on SchoolTrack that could set cookies on your device.

If you have any questions, please contact us.
