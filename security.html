Security & Data Privacy

Your data and your students' data are safe with SchoolTrack.

Local-first storage — All data is stored in your browser using localStorage. No student information is ever sent to our servers.

No tracking — SchoolTrack uses no cookies, no analytics, no advertising scripts, and no third-party integrations that could access your data.

Your control — Export your data any time. Delete it any time. You are in complete control of everything stored in SchoolTrack.

Responsible use — As the school administrator, you are responsible for ensuring that student data is handled appropriately under your local privacy laws. Because data stays on your device, you maintain direct custody of all information.

Password security — We recommend using a strong, unique password for your SchoolTrack account and keeping your device protected.
