Built for Islamic schools

SchoolTrack is a school management platform designed specifically for Islamic schools, madrasas, and maktabs. We believe every Muslim educational institution deserves modern tools that respect their unique structure and values.

Managing an Islamic school is unlike managing any other school. You have Hifz students progressing through the 30 Juz, maktab programs running evenings and weekends, and families who deserve clear communication in the language of trust. Generic software was never built with this in mind — SchoolTrack was.

We started SchoolTrack because we saw too many masjid schools still running on spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and paper registers. We wanted to change that — starting with something simple, focused, and free to use.
