Documentation & Guides

Everything you need to get the most out of SchoolTrack.

Getting Started — Create your account, then add your classes and enroll students. The demo data pre-loaded will show you how each feature works.

Students — Add and manage student profiles, track enrollment status, and view individual grade and attendance records.

Attendance — Take daily attendance by class. Mark students Present, Absent, or Late. Save all at once with one click.

Gradebook — Log grades by subject and assignment. View student averages and overall class performance.

Hifz Tracking — Log daily sessions with sabaq, sabqi, and manzil. Track how many Juz each student has completed.

Messages — Record messages sent to parents, teachers, or student groups. Keep a log of all announcements.

Settings — Update your school name, export a backup of all your data, or reset the app.

Data & Backups — All data is stored in your browser. Use Settings → Export Data to download a JSON backup regularly.
